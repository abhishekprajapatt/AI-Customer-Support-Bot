**Demo Script: AI Customer Support Bot**

**🎤 Introduction:**
Hello everyone! We are Team AbhishekPrajapati2006, and today we are excited to present our project: **AI Customer Support Bot.** This solution aims to revolutionize customer service by providing instant, intelligent, and scalable support using AI.

---

**📝 Problem Statement:**
Traditional customer support systems have several issues:
- Long wait times
- High operational costs
- Inconsistent responses 
- Difficulty in scaling support teams

Our AI-powered chatbot eliminates these inefficiencies and enhances the user experience.

---

**💡 Solution Overview:**
Our bot uses **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** to understand user queries and provide instant, accurate responses. It seamlessly integrates with websites and mobile apps, ensuring businesses can offer **24/7 support** without additional costs.

---

**⚙️ Tech Stack:**
- **Frontend:** React.js (Web) & React Native (Mobile)
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
- **Database:** SQLite
- **Authentication:** OTP-based login, Fingerprint auth
- **AI Model:** NLP-based chatbot
- **Hosting:** GitHub, Vercel, AWS

---

**🚀 Key Features:**
✅ AI-powered chatbot for real-time customer support
✅ Multi-platform accessibility (Web & Mobile)
✅ Secure authentication (OTP & Fingerprint)
✅ User-friendly dashboard for managing queries
✅ Message, Stories, Notifications & Analytics
✅ Scalable backend using SQLite database

---

**📽️ Demo Walkthrough:**
1. **Login & Authentication:** 
   - Users sign in using **email/phone OTP or fingerprint.**
2. **Chatbot Interaction:** 
   - Users ask queries, and the AI chatbot responds instantly.
3. **Dashboard Features:**
   - Users can view chat history, notifications, and analytics.
4. **Real-time Data Storage:**
   - SQLite database stores and retrieves user queries efficiently.
5. **Mobile App Functionality:**
   - The chatbot is accessible via both **web and mobile platforms.**
